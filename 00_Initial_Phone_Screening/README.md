# Google Initial Phone Screening Strategy

This guide is specifically tailored for **Aayush Gupta** (Tech Lead at TCS, 4+ years of experience in Java, Spring Boot, Microservices, Angular, React, and Azure) for the initial phone screening call with the Google APAC Recruitment Team (Recruiter: Shreyash).

## 🎯 What Happens in a Google Initial Phone Screening?
Typically lasts **20–30 minutes**. The recruiter evaluates:
*   Communication clarity
*   Confidence
*   Problem-solving exposure
*   Resume authenticity
*   Motivation for joining Google

**Recruiter may ask:**
*   Tell me about yourself.
*   Current role & tech stack.
*   Projects you worked on (especially impactful ones).
*   Why do you want to join Google?
*   Role preference (Application Engineer, Backend, Full-stack, etc.).
*   Location flexibility & Notice period.
*   (Sometimes) Salary expectations.
*   (Sometimes) Coding fundamentals and DSA preparation level.

## 🙋 Questions YOU Should Ask the Recruiter
At the end, when they ask, "Do you have any questions?", ask:
1. Which team is this role for?
2. What skills should I focus on for the next rounds?
3. What is the exact structure of the interview process?
4. Is DSA heavily required for this specific application engineer role?
